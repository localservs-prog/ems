About EMS

EMS (Economy Moving Service LLC) is a professional moving and logistics company based in Los Angeles, California. We specialize in residential and commercial relocation services with an AI-powered customer communication platform.

Founded to modernize the moving industry, EMS combines logistics expertise with intelligent automation. Our platform provides real-time booking confirmations, delivery status updates, driver notifications, and promotional offers — delivered via SMS to our customers.

We are committed to transparency, reliability, and customer satisfaction in every move we manage.
